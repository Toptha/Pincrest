About Pincrest

Our Mission

Pincrest's mission is to bring everyone the inspiration to create a life they love.

We believe that everyone deserves to find ideas that excite them and help them become the best version of themselves.

What is Pincrest?

Pincrest is a visual discovery engine for finding ideas like recipes, home and style inspiration, and more. With billions of Pins on Pinterest, you'll always find ideas to spark inspiration. When you discover Pins you love, save them to boards to keep your ideas organized and easy to find.

Our History

2010: Pincrest is founded by Ben Silbermann, Paul Sciarra, and Evan Sharp.
2011: The Pincrest iOS app is launched.
2012: Pincrest opens to the public without requiring an invitation.
2015: Buyable Pins are introduced, allowing users to purchase products directly through the platform.
2019: Pincrest goes public, listing on the New York Stock Exchange.

Pincrest's Impact

Pincrest has become a powerful platform for inspiration, creativity, and commerce. It helps millions of people discover new ideas, plan their projects, and find products they love. For businesses, Pincrest serves as a unique marketing platform, allowing them to reach engaged audiences at various stages of the purchase decision-making process.
